LION BRAND®VELVET SPUN®
Knit Baby Hat
Pattern number 40337
Skill Level: Easy
SIZE
6 month (12 month)
Circumference 14.5 (17.25)” [36.75 (43.75) cm]
MATERIALS
• LION BRAND Velvet Spun
1 ball #106 Bluebell
or color of your choice
• LION BRAND size 10.5 (6.5 mm) knitting needles OR SIZE TO OBTAIN GAUGE
• LION BRAND large-eyed blunt needle
GAUGE
9 stitches + 14 rows = 4” (10 cm) in Stockinette stitch (knit on right side, purl on wrong
side).
BE SURE TO CHECK YOUR GAUGE.
HAT
Cast on 33 (39) stitches. Work in St st until piece measures 4” [10 cm] from beginning,
ending with a wrong side row.
CROWN
Decrease Row (right side) Knit 2 together, knit to end – 32 (38) sts.
Purl 1 row.
Decrease Row (right side) K 1, *k 4, k2tog; repeat from *, end k 1 – 27 (32) sts.
Purl 1 row.
Decrease Row K 1, *k 3, k2tog; repeat from *, end k 1 – 22 (26) sts.
Purl 1 row.
Decrease Row K 1, * k 2, k2tog; repeat from *, end k 1 – 17 (20) sts.
Purl 1 row.
Decrease Row K 1, * k 1, k2tog; repeat from *, end k 1 – 12 (14) sts.
Purl 1 row.
Decrease Row * K2tog; repeat from *– 6 (7) sts.
Next Row *Purl 2 together; repeat from *, end k 0 (1) – 3 (4) sts.
Larger size only K2tog, k 2 – 3 stitches.
Top Knot Cord
Continue to k every row on 3 sts, at end of row slipping sts back onto left-hand needle to
work next row, until cord measures 5” [12.5 cm].

Last Row K 1, k2tog, pass the k st over the k2tog st. Fasten off.
Sew back seam. Weave in ends.
ABBREVIATIONS:
k = knit st(s) = stitch(es)
k2tog = knit 2 together St st = stockinette stitch
