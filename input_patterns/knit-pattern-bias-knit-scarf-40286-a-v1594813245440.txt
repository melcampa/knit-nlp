LION BRAND®MOONLIGHT MOHAIR
Bias Knit Scarf
Page 1
Pattern from Lion Brand Yarn Page 2
Skill Level: Beginner
SIZE
5” x 36” Note: scarf will stretch with wear
MATERIALS
• Lion Brand Moonlight Mohair
1 ball #205 Glacier Bay or color of your choice
• LION BRAND size 13 (9 mm) knitting needles OR SIZE TO OBTAIN GAUGE
• LION BRAND large-eyed, blunt needle
GAUGE
12 stitches (measured along bias row; Note: final scarf width above is measured
horizontally across body of scarf, not diagonally along the bias) + 16 rows (8 ridges) = 4”
(10 cm) in garter stitch (knit every row).
BE SURE TO CHECK YOUR GAUGE.
Scarf
Cast on 18 stitches.
Row 1 Slip first stitch as if to purl. Knit into front and back of next stitch (increase 1).
Knit until 3 stitches remain on needle, knit 2 together (decrease 1), knit 1 – 18 stitches.
Row 2 Slip first stitch as if to purl. Knit to end.
Repeat Rows 1 and 2 until about 24” of yarn remains. Bind off loosely and weave in
ends.
Moonlight Mohair (Article #510) is packaged in 1 3/4 oz/82 yd balls
