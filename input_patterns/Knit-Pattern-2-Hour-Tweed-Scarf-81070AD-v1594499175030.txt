Free Knitting Pattern from Lion Brand Yarn
Lion Brand® Nature's Choice ® Organic Cotton
2 Hour Tweed Scarf
Pattern Number: 81070AD
SKILL LEVEL: Beginner
SIZE: One Size
About 8 x 60 in. (20.5 x 152.5 cm)
CORRECTIONS: None
MATERIALS
• 480-169 Lion Brand Nature's Choice Organic Cotton: Dusty Sage
1 Ball (A)
• 480-174 Lion Brand Nature's Choice Organic Cotton: Olive
1 Ball (B)
• 480-136 Lion Brand Nature's Choice Organic Cotton: Spice
1 Ball (C)
• Lion Brand Knitting Needles - size 35 [19mm]
• Large-Eyed Blunt Needles
GAUGE:
5 stitches = 4 in. (10 cm) in Garter stitch (knit every row) with 1 strand each of A, B,
and C held together BE SURE TO CHECK YOUR GAUGE. When you match the gauge in
a pattern, your project will be the size specified in the pattern and the materials
specified in the pattern will be sufficient. If it takes you less stitches and rows to make
a 4 in. [10 cm] square, try using a smaller size hook or needles; if more stitches and
rows, try a larger size hook or needles.
NOTES:
Scarf is worked with 1 strand each of A, B, and C held together throughout.
SCARF
With 1 strand each of A, B, and C held together, cast on 14 stitches.
Work in Garter stitch (knit every row) until piece measures about 60 in. (152.5 cm) from beginning.
Bind off.
FINISHING
Weave in ends.
