GAUGE:
16 stitches = 4 in. (10 cm) in Stockinette stitch (knit on right side, purl on wrong side).
SCARF
Cast on 22 stitches.
Work in Stockinette stitch (knit on right side, purl on wrong side) until almost all yarn has been used.
Bind off.
FINISHING
Weave in ends.
