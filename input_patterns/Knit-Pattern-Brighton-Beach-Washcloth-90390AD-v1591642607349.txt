1 Ball (C)
• Lion Brand Knitting Needles - Size 7 [4.5 mm]
• Large-Eye Blunt Needles (Set of 6)
GAUGE:
Exact gauge is not important to this project
NOTES:
Carry colors not in use along side edge of work.
STRIPE SEQUENCE *Work 2 rows each with A, B and C; rep from * for Stripe Sequence.
WASHCLOTH
With A, cast on 5 sts. Knit 1 row.
Next Row: K2, yo, k to end of row.
Rep last row, changing color following Stripe Sequence, until you have a total of 52 sts.
Next Row: K2, yo, (k2tog) twice, k to end of row.
Rep last row until 5 sts remain. K 1 row. Bind off.
FINISHING
Weave in ends.
ABBREVIATIONS / REFERENCES
