Lion Brand® Lion Cotton - Fun Fur
Cell Phone Case
Pattern Number: 50247
SKILL LEVEL: Beginner
SIZE: One Size
3.5 inches approximately
CORRECTIONS: None
MATERIALS
• 760-174 Lion Brand Lion Cotton: Lime
1 Skeins (A)
• 320-215 Lion Brand Fun Fur: Capri
1 Skeins (B)
• Lion Brand Knitting Needles- Size 6 [4.00 mm]
• Lion Brand Double-Pointed Needles - Size 6
• Large-Eye Blunt Needles (Set of 6)
• Additional Materials
1. One Size 4 Snap
2. needle and thread
GAUGE:
16 sts + 24 rows = 4 in [10cm]. BE SURE TO CHECK YOUR GAUGE.
STITCH EXPLANATION:
SEED STITCH (multiple of 2)
Row 1 *K1, p1; rep from *
Row 2 *P1, k1; rep from *
CASE
FRONT
With (A) cast on 10 sts. Work in Seed st until piece measures 3½" [9cm] from beg. Bind off.
BACK
Work as for Front to last (bind off) row. Bind off all but last 4 sts. Work these 4 sts in Seed st to make Strap
5½” [14cm] long. Bind off.
FINISHING
Sew side and bottom seams. Sew snap onto Case and onto end of Strap. With (B) and double pointed
needles pick up 20 sts around top of Case. P 1 round. K 1 round. Bind off. Weave in ends.
