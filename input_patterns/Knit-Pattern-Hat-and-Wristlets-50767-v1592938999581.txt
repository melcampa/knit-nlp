Free Knitting Pattern from Lion Brand Yarn
Lion Brand® Fettuccini®
Hat and Wristlets
Pattern Number: 50767
SKILL LEVEL: Easy
SIZE: One Size
Hat 20 inches [51 cm] circumference
Wristlets 13 inches [33 cm] long
CORRECTIONS: (applied Jan 30, 2013)
HAT
Cast on 30 sts. Work in Garter st for 2½ inches [6.5 cm]; end with a WS row. Purl 2 rows. Work 4
inches [10 cm] in St st (k on RS, p on WS).
Dec Row (RS) K 1, k2tog, knit to center st, k2tog, k to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1 – 27 sts.
Work even until piece measures 6 inches [15 cm] from beg Garter st; end with a WS row. Rep Dec Row
– 24 sts. Work even until piece measures 7 inches [18 cm] from beg Garter st; end with a WS row. Rep
Dec Row – 21 sts. Continue in St st until piece measures 8 inches [20.5 cm] from beg Garter st; end
with a WS row.
MATERIALS
• 290-201 Lion Brand Fettuccini Yarn: Eden
4 Balls 2 skeins each, hat and wristlets
• Lion Brand Knitting Needles - Size 19 [15 mm]
• Lion Brand Large-Eye Blunt Needles (Set of 6)
GAUGE:
6 sts + 12 rows = 4 inches [10 cm] in Garter st (k every row).
BE SURE TO CHECK YOUR GAUGE. When you match the gauge in a pattern, your project will be the
size specified in the pattern and the materials specified in the pattern will be sufficient. If it takes you
fewer stitches and rows to make a 4 in. [10 cm] square, try using a smaller size hook or needles; if
more stitches and rows, try a larger size hook or needles.
HAT
Cast on 30 sts. Work in Garter st for 2½ inches [6.5 cm]; end with a WS row. Purl 2 rows. Work 4 inches [10 cm] in
St st (k on RS, p on WS).
Dec Row (RS) K 1, k2tog, knit to center st, k2tog, k to last 3 sts, k2tog, k 1 – 27 sts.
Work even until piece measures 6 inches [15 cm] from Garter st; end with a WS row. Rep Dec Row – 24 sts. Work
even until piece measures 7 inches [18 cm] from Garter st; end with a WS row. Rep Dec Row – 21 sts. Continue in
St st until piece measures 8 inches [20.5 cm] from Garter st; end with a WS row.
Last Row (RS) (K2tog) 10 times, k 1 – 11 sts.
Cut yarn, leaving a 15 inches [38 cm] tail.
FINISHING
With large-eyed blunt needle, thread yarn through remaining 11 sts; pull tightly and secure; then sew seam. Weave
in ends.
WRISTLETS
Cast on 12 sts. Work in Garter st for 3 inches [7.5 cm]. Dec 1 st each end of next row – 10 sts. Continue even in
Garter st until piece measures 7 inches [18 cm] from beg. Increase 1 st each end of next row – 12 sts. Continue
even in Garter st until piece measures 13 inches [33 cm] from beg. Bind off loosely. Cut yarn, leaving a 24 inches
[61 cm] tail.
FINISHING
With large-eyed blunt needle, sew 9½ inches [24 cm] of seam closed. Leave next 2 inches [5 cm] open for thumb,
and sew remaining 1½ inches [4 cm] seam. Weave in ends.
